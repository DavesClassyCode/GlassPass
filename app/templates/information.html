Glass Pass

The platform is designed to simplify the entire booking process, from selecting the preferred date and time to making a secure payment, all in one easy-to-use platform. With its intuitive design and powerful features, Glass Pass is the ideal solution for anyone looking for a convenient and efficient way to book and pay for ice rinks.
